## Parameters
<Cards>
  <Card title="user (required)" description="User to remove the role from" />
  <Card title="role (required)" description="The temporary role to remove" />
  <Card title="reason (optional)" description="Reason for removal" />
</Cards>

## Step-by-Step Example
1. Identify the user and role to remove.<br />
2. Run the command:
    ```bash
    /remove-temp-role 
    user:@Username 
    role:@VIP 
    reason:"Early removal requested"
    ```
3. The bot will remove the temporary role from the user.

## Troubleshooting
<Callout type="warning">
- **Role not removed:** Ensure the bot has Manage Roles permission and the role is temporary.<br />
- **User not found:** Double-check the username or mention.
</Callout>
